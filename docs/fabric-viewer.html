Load Your Fabric

Drop a fabric.json file here, or click to browse.

Find it at: ~/.ecphory/projects/{project}/fabric.json

Pipe fabric data from the CLI

# Export a project's fabric as JSON:
intent fabric export --project myproject > fabric.json

# Or copy the raw fabric file:
cp ~/.ecphory/myproject/fabric.json .

# Then drag fabric.json onto this viewer,
# or click the upload area above.
